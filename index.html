About the Creator

As the title of this section states, it is a little bit about who I am. I will not use my given name in this section for the sake of privacy. If you happen upon this page you should know my name. I am twenty-two years of age and was born and raised in Kansas, which is where I currently live as well. I am a fitness enthusiast. I enjoy most cardio based activities with a focus on running and swimming. I originally pursued swimming due to an interest to pursue a career in Air Force Special Warfare (AFSPECWAR). Within that career field I was mainly interested in becoming a CCT or a PJ. I have since opted not to pursue that career field. If you would like to know why, please feel free to reach out. Back to swimming. In the beginning I found it to be extremely challenging both physically but mainly mentally. My water confidence was found to be lacking, badly. I have been working at it for a little over a year now and can swim 1500+ meters without stopping. It has vastly improved my cardiovascular endurance as a side effect. I recently completed a half marathon in 1:38:24. At the end I wasn't really winded but my muscles were fatigued badly. I have spoken a great deal on cardio based excercise. I also enjoy weight lifting and mainly body weight exercises. I do a lot of push-ups and pull-ups.

I have spoken a great deal on fitness, but it is my favorite hobby to be sure. I also enjoy playing video games, though I don't play as much as I did in my younger years. I am a fan of the Scfi-fantasy genre. My favorite authors are probably Michael J. Sullivan, Larry Correia, and Jim Butcher to name a few. Well, that is a little about me.
